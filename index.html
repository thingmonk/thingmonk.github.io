Venue Information


Eclipse Day : Protein, entry via Cream, 31 New Inn Yard, EC2A 3EY

ThingMonk is the developer conference for people making IoT platforms. We bring together technologists and designers building core infrastructure for IoT for 2 days of awesome, unique talks on topics including bots, industrial automation/industry 4.0, machine learning, time series data, programming language choices and UX. These are talks by practitioners.

We’re not cheerleaders for thoughtless automation- we’ll have skeptical, thoughtful critical talks about creating a culture and making the right design decisions so that the IoT serves us, rather than us serving it. Run screaming for Internet-connected toasters.

ThingMonk is about tech but it’s also a warm, supportive, welcoming and diverse community. This year we are honoured to introduce a special diversity scholarship sponsored by OpenSensors.io.

ThingMonk 2016 will be held at two venues in Shoreditch within 150 yards of each - Day 0 at Cream/Protein and the hack day, Day 1 and Day 2 at Shoreditch Studioss

Registration for will be open from 9AM at Cream, 31 New Inn Yard, EC2A 3EY for the morning of Day 0, and at Shoreditch Studios, 37 Batemans Row, EC2A 3HH for the ThingMonk Hack Day on the afternoon of Day 0, Day 1 and Day 2.

ThingMonk 2016 cannot occur without the generous of our friends in the IoT Community. If you would like to be a sponsor of the premier IoT event in London click here.

We'll definitely have some Echos to play with, and expect a couple of giveaways too. This won't be a competitive event, but a great place to learn, meet people and build cool things.
